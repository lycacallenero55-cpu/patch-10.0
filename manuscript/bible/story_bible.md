## Core Rules
**[TAG: FRANCHISE_CANON — TASK-0004 proposal row 4]**

1. **Patches rewrite everything** — geography, history, memories. Seamless retcon. Only he persists.
2. **Anchor rule:** whatever he *holds/carries* when the wave hits, persists (one bag). **Living beings cannot be anchored** — tried once (Captain Go, 5.0→6.0): the wave "split the difference"; what came through was neither man. Never again.
3. **Carry-over law:** only what is flesh/soul-deep survives in HIM (qi core, god-brand, neural implant). External gear converts/deletes.
4. **Deprecation law (10.0+):** legacy powers are garbage-collected on use — every burn is permanent loss. His nine builds = finite, non-renewable vault. Every unseal is a funeral.
5. **Lost Safety Net:** every patch used to repair his body (his true insurance). 10.0 is FINAL — from Ch.3 onward all wounds/losses/deaths (his included) are permanent.
6. **No resurrections. Ever.** Injuries persist for all characters.
7. Old powers are "unindexed" → using them draws **Moderators**.
