## 2 · COMPLETE STORY
### Premise
Reality patches itself ~every 10 years — a full version update rewriting geography, physics, institutions, and every memory so the new world "has always existed." One person persists: born in Version 1.0, now ~92 in a ~20-year-old body, currently named **Seo Han**. Nine worlds lived (1.0–9.0), eight patches endured. The story opens 3 days before patch nine — which announces itself, uniquely, as **VERSION 10.0: FINAL PATCH — EXTINCTION GENRE**, and for the first time speaks to him: *"Are you still there?"*
