## Impact & motion grammar (video)
- IMPACT FRAMES: 1–2 frame full-screen inversion (white-on-black linework) at heavy hits — max 2 per shot.
- Speed lines carry velocity; feet DIG (debris kicks) on acceleration; recoil on every block; NO floaty weightlessness, NO jelly limbs (re-roll on sight).
- Slow-motion: max one 0.5s ramp per shot, always at a decision point (blade meeting, eyes narrowing).
- Smoke/dust: flat 2D shapes, hard edges. Rain: straight streaks. Blood: dark maroon spatter, never fountains.
- Sound pairing: Han = silence then one clean sound; enemies = loud; system = clinical tones; grief = room tone only.
