## Environmental design language
9.0 = soft-genre coziness: warm woods, paper lanterns, trams, plane trees; nothing brutalist. 8.0 = rust, patched timber, tarps, dust. 10.0 = 9.0's bones + wrongness: debris of older worlds intruding, colors drained, one accent light per scene. Architecture never randomizes: reuse plate geometry exactly; extend off-screen logically.
