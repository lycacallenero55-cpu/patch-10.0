# Validation Profile
**Status: VALIDATION_UNDEFINED** — no build, test, lint, or link-check tooling exists in this repository. No validation is claimed.
What exists instead (manual/agent checks):
- Markdown: rendered-eyeball only; no linter configured.
- Readers (HTML): render in a sandboxed iframe on the platform; no automated check in-repo. Image URLs inside readers are platform-hosted (see INCIDENTS I-0001 durability note).
- Media: probed ad hoc with ffprobe in the production sandbox; not reproducible from this repo alone.
- **Per-commit lane verification (added CYCLE-0001):** during a Creator-authorized Conductor cycle, ① dispatches one department at a time and verifies each department's resulting commit on GitHub (via `github__get_commit`'s changed-file list) against Amendment 1 §A1.2's write-lane ownership map before dispatching the next department. This is a manual/agent check performed per-turn during conducted cycles, not a CI gate — it depends on ① (or ⑤, retroactively) actually running it, and does not run automatically on every push outside a conducted cycle.
- **⑤ cycle-end lane and content audit (added CYCLE-0001, TASK-0016):** at the end of a conducted cycle, ⑤ Quality Assurance independently re-verifies every commit's changed-file list via `github__get_commit`, spot-checks a sample of new/changed content against its cited sources (e.g. adaptation-variance claims checked against the actual prose/script/reader text; continuity-micro-rule citations checked against `studio/00_STUDIO_RULES.md` §5 and `CONTINUITY_LEDGER.md`), and reports findings in `studio/06_Operations/REVIEWS/`. This is retrospective and manual — it happens once per completed cycle, not continuously, and its coverage is bounded by what the dispatching work order names as in-scope (this cycle: 6 of TASK-0008's ~28 variance rows were directly spot-checked verbatim against source; the remainder were read but not individually re-verified against a primary source).
- **SHA spot-checks by departments in their own handoffs (observed CYCLE-0001):** individual departments (this cycle: ④) have independently cross-referenced `github__list_commits`/`get_commit` output against their own or a prior turn's `STUDIO_STATUS.md` claims when something looked stale, and disclosed the discrepancy in their handoff rather than silently trusting the file. This is opportunistic, not systematic — it happened because a department noticed something, not because a check runs automatically.
Proposed (not created — needs creator interest): a tiny CI that link-checks Markdown and validates internal anchors. Until then, never claim tests passed.
