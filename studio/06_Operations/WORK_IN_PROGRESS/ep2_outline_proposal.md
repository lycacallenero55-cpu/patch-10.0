## 2. Beat-by-beat outline

Thirteen beats, sequenced to cover Chapter 2's full compressed span (Day −3 morning through Day −3 midnight) in a single episode, mirroring EP.1's own one-chapter-per-episode cadence. Each beat names its grounding source; beats marked "(PROPOSAL — connective only)" are outline-only sequencing/pacing suggestions with no independent canon claim, analogous to the `ADDED_IN_ADAPTATION`/`ADAPTATION_CONNECTIVE` categories `ep1_adaptation_variance.md` already documents as sanctioned adaptation technique.

1. **Cold open — "72 hours, one bag."** Han packing/checking the bag and its eight bundles at dawn; establishes the Day −3 countdown visually before any dialogue. *Source: `CONTINUITY_LEDGER.md` baseline (bag + 8 bundles); `readers/episode1_part2.html` endcard framing ("seventy-two hours, one bag to pack") adopted as the episode's own cold-open register, consistent with EP.1's own in-medias-res cold-open technique documented in `ep1_adaptation_variance.md`'s first table row.*
2. **Unnoticed goodbyes (montage).** Han moving through ordinary Day −3 academy life — classmates, routine — each interaction quietly a goodbye only he recognizes as final, unnoticed by everyone else. *Source: `ep1_v3_script.md` EP2 PREVIEW ("unnoticed goodbyes"); consistent with `story_bible.md`'s MC's-want section and the "stranger not liar" characterization already adapted in EP.1.*
3. **Ha-eun's manhwa debut — the library, the humming, "haven't we met, senior."** Full adaptation of prose Ch.1's library scene (bell-tower measurement detail, the un-indexable melody, her suspicion that she's met him before). This is the episode's largest new-character introduction. *Source: `manuscript/chapters/01_the_ninth_world.md`'s Ha-eun library scene (verbatim text exists and should be pulled directly, per `ep1_adaptation_variance.md`'s explicit recommendation); `CONTINUITY_LEDGER.md` ("Yoo Ha-eun: introduced in prose Ch.1 only (manhwa debut = EP.2); measured 3 bell towers flat; hums the un-indexable melody; suspects she's met Han"); `Master_Project_Bible.md` Characters section ("Debuts EP.2").*
4. **Professor Im Dan-bi's introduction — the warmth beat.** A single scene establishing Im Dan-bi's character through kindness/warmth, per the locked beat description, without foreshadowing any specific fate. *Source: `Master_Project_Bible.md` locked Ch.2 beats ("Professor Im Dan-bi's warmth"); `story_bible.md` Cast section (introduced Ch.2). This outline deliberately does NOT stage Im Dan-bi in any scene that implies or forecloses the Q-01 first-death question — see §4 below.*
5. **Si-woo continuity thread — the dream, unresolved.** A brief callback beat keeping Si-woo's recurring 8.0 dream (established in EP.1) visibly alive without advancing or explaining it, so the "leak" thread doesn't go silent for a full episode. *(PROPOSAL — connective only; no independent source claims this specific beat occurs in Ch.2, but nothing in `CONTINUITY_LEDGER.md` forbids it, and EP.1's ending explicitly left this thread open. (PROPOSAL for placement/length only — the underlying fact is confirmed canon, its inclusion here as a discrete beat is a sequencing suggestion.)*
7. **Midday — festival prep escalates.** Visual/atmosphere beat: banners, stalls, academy-wide festival-prep energy rising, setting up both the headmaster's speech (Beat 9) and the midnight tear (Beat 13) against a backdrop the reader can feel accelerating. *Source: `CONTINUITY_LEDGER.md` ("Environment: Haeseong festival prep ongoing; banners up"); `story_bible.md` 9.0 World quick canon (Founding Festival ~3 days at Ch.1 end).*
8. **The keepsake funeral — eight bundles eulogized.** Han privately goes through all eight keepsake bundles one by one; per the locked beat, this is a eulogy structure — each bundle gets its due — building toward the hairpin. *Source: `Master_Project_Bible.md` locked Ch.2 beats ("the keepsake funeral (8 bundles eulogized...)"); `story_bible.md` Keepsakes list (all eight items, in order).*

## 3. Adaptation notes (pacing, part/strip mapping, cliffhanger placement)

This section references, but does not modify, the existing strip-model-v5 production doctrine already locked in the ③ Visual lane (`studio/02_Art/Art_Direction.md` and related files, not opened or edited by this outline).

- **Two-part structure, mirroring EP.1.** EP.1 shipped as `episode1_part1.html` + `episode1_part2.html`. This outline recommends the same two-part split for EP.2, since Chapter 2's own compressed structure (per `story_bible.md`'s drafting log: "goodbyes, Im Dan-bi shine, headmaster speech w/ planted sentence, keepsake funeral + hairpin silence, 9.0 keepsake chosen, midnight tear + banner") divides naturally around the festival-speech beat (Beat 11): **Part 1 = Beats 1–8** (cold open through the keepsake funeral's eulogy build-up, ending on a hush before the hairpin), **Part 2 = Beats 9–13** (the hairpin's silence through the midnight tear and the banner's question). This mirrors EP.1's own Part 1/Part 2 break, which fell at a comparable emotional pivot (the sky-tear/rewrite event), giving the two episodes a consistent internal rhythm for readers.
  - This is a pacing PROPOSAL only; the exact strip/panel count and part-break point are ③ Visual's call at script/storyboard time, per Amendment 1 §A1.2's lane ownership (readers/ and 02_Art/ belong to ③, not ②). This outline offers the split as a structural suggestion grounded in the chapter's own compression pattern, not as an instruction.
- **Cliffhanger placement.** Beat 13 (the sky tear + the banner's question) is proposed as the episode's final beat and Part 2's closer, matching EP.1's own convention of ending on the sharpest possible turn (EP.1 ended on the "running early" realization, one beat before the prose chapter's actual final paragraph, per `ep1_adaptation_variance.md`'s last row — a deliberate punchier-cliffhanger pacing choice). This outline recommends EP.2 follow the same convention: end exactly on the banner's question, without continuing into whatever Han does or thinks next (that belongs to Ch.3 / EP.3).
- **Ha-eun's debut needs its own visual weight.** Since Beat 3 is carrying a full deferred character introduction (documented as the single largest omission from EP.1 per `ep1_adaptation_variance.md` synthesis finding #1), this outline recommends the eventual script/storyboard treat it as a full scene, not a compressed montage panel — consistent with how EP.1 gave Ha-eun's prose equivalent (Si-woo's dream reveal) a comparably weighted multi-panel treatment rather than a single-panel summary.
- **The keepsake funeral (Beats 8–10) is the episode's emotional core and should not be rushed into the festival-speech beat.** Per the locked beat description, eight bundles are eulogized individually before the hairpin's silence and the 9.0 choice — this argues for the funeral sequence getting proportionally more strip-space than a single-page summary, similar to how EP.1's noodle-shop scene (also multi-beat, callus + tip + "I'll be quick") received extended treatment rather than compression into one panel.
- **No lettering/bubble/CSS convention is proposed to change.** This outline assumes the existing strip-model-v5 block/gap physics and bubble/lettering conventions apply unchanged to EP.2; nothing here asks ③/④ to revisit locked art or engine decisions.
